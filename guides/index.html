HIPAA Compliance & Data Privacy

All tools that handle Protected Health Information (PHI) operate under a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with Childhood Interventions, LLC. Each guide below includes a dedicated HIPAA & Privacy section explaining exactly where data is stored, what leaves your browser, and what responsibilities you carry as the provider. Never store PHI in personal cloud storage (iCloud, personal Google Drive, personal Dropbox) without a BAA in place.
