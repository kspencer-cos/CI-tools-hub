Overview

The Periodic & Annual Review Tool takes the most time-consuming part of an IFSP review — writing progress summaries — and turns it into a guided, AI-assisted process. Upload the session notes from the review period, and the tool generates a narrative summary for each of the three IFSP outcome areas.

A
Positive Social-Emotional Skills
B
Acquiring & Using Knowledge & Skills
C
Taking Appropriate Action to Meet Needs

The tool organizes generated content around the three federal IFSP outcome areas (A, B, C) so the summaries are ready to paste directly into IFSP review documentation.

Uploading Session Notes

The tool accepts session notes in PDF or text format. You can upload multiple notes from the review period at once — the AI reads all of them to build the progress summary.

1
Gather the session notes for the review period
Collect all completed session notes from the current IFSP period. These can be PDFs downloaded from EI Session Notes, scanned paper notes, or text documents. The more complete the notes, the better the summary.
2
Drag and drop or browse to upload
On the main screen, drag your note files onto the upload area or click Browse files. Multiple files can be selected at once. Supported formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT.
3
Enter review details
Enter the child's initials (not full name), date of birth, review period start and end dates, and any specific goals or outcomes to focus on. Use initials and dates — not identifying names.
⚠️
Use initials, not full names. Enter the child's initials (e.g., "J.S.") in the name field — not their full name. The tool redacts obvious identifiers before sending to AI, but minimizing PHI in input fields is best practice and your responsibility.
🤖
Generating Summaries

Once notes are uploaded, the AI reads through all of them and generates a progress narrative for each IFSP outcome area.

1
Click "Generate Summary"
After uploading notes and entering review details, click Generate Summary. Processing typically takes 20–60 seconds depending on the number and length of notes.
2
Wait for the progress indicator
A progress bar shows while the notes are processed. Do not close the tab — if you navigate away, you'll need to restart. The tool processes notes locally in your browser before sending anonymized content to the AI.
3
Review the generated summaries
Three summary sections appear — one per IFSP outcome area. Each is an editable text block. Read each carefully before use.
🎯
Outcome Areas

The tool generates a progress narrative for each of Florida's three IFSP outcome areas, aligned with ESDS documentation requirements.

💛
Outcome A — Social-Emotional
Summarizes progress in positive social relationships, emotional regulation, and social communication with peers and adults.
📘
Outcome B — Knowledge & Skills
Documents acquisition of concepts, vocabulary, early literacy/numeracy, problem solving, and imitation from the review period notes.
💚
Outcome C — Meeting Needs
Covers the child's ability to take care of basic needs: feeding, dressing, mobility, safety, and communicating wants and needs.
💡
COS rating connection: Progress summaries generated here can also be used as source material when completing a Child Outcome Summary (COS) rating in the COS Scoring Tool. Both tools are designed to work together in your IFSP review workflow.
✏️
Editing & Finalizing

AI-generated summaries are a starting point — always review and personalize before using in official documentation.

⚠️
Review all generated text before use. AI can miss context, misinterpret clinical language, or generalize incorrectly. You are the clinician of record — the final summary is your professional statement and must reflect your accurate clinical judgment.
✏️
Click into any summary block to edit
Each outcome area is an editable text field. Click directly into the text to make changes. Your edits are not auto-saved — copy your final text or use the export function before closing.
🔄
Regenerate if needed
If a section doesn't capture the right themes, you can regenerate individual sections or all three. Each regeneration may produce slightly different language.
💾
Saving & Exporting

The tool does not auto-save summaries to a cloud account — export your completed summaries before closing the browser.

WhatHow to saveWhere it goes
Summary text Copy to clipboard or Export PDF Your device or clipboard
Uploaded note files Not stored — processed in memory only Never leave your browser session
Your subscription Managed via email validation Childhood Interventions servers (email only)
📥
Export to PDF: Click Export PDF to download a formatted summary document. Store the PDF in a HIPAA-compliant location — do not save to personal cloud storage (iCloud, personal Google Drive, personal Dropbox) without a BAA in place.
📋
Copy to IFSP documentation: You can also copy the text from each outcome area directly into your IFSP review form in ESDS or another system. The text is yours to use in any format your program requires.

HIPAA & Privacy
🛡️
PHI is redacted before AI processing
Before any note content is sent to the AI, the child's name is replaced with initials and the Medicaid number with [ID-REDACTED] — both done locally in your browser. The tool does not store or retain session note content. Avoid pasting other identifiers such as caregiver names or addresses into session note fields.
📥
Notes stay in your browser
Uploaded session note files are read in your browser and never uploaded to our servers. The files go from your device to the AI — not through our storage.
🔐
Subscription auth only
The only data stored server-side is your email address and subscription status. No notes, summaries, or child data are ever stored on our servers.
🌐
HTTPS throughout
All connections are encrypted via HTTPS. Do not use the tool on public Wi-Fi without a VPN, as your screen content could be visible to others nearby.

Your responsibilities:

  • Use initials only (not full child names) when entering review details.
  • Only use the tool on a device with a screen lock and password protection.
  • Export PDFs to HIPAA-compliant storage locations only.
  • Do not share your subscription email/login with others outside your organization.
  • Log out or close the browser tab when finished, especially on shared devices.
