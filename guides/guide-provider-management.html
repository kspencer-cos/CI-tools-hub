Overview

Provider Management connects your Google Sheets billing spreadsheet to give you a live command center for your Early Steps practice. All your billing, scheduling, and caseload data lives in your own Google Drive — nothing is stored on our servers.

🔄
ESDS Reconcile
Import a ClaimDetailExtract.xlsx from ESDS and instantly see what's changed, missing, or flagged — apply updates to your CONT tab in one click.
👨‍👩‍👦
Caseload
Track auth expiration dates, periodic and annual review due dates, and caregiver contact info for your entire active caseload.
📅
Weekly Schedule
Live calendar view pulled from Google Calendar — shows session status including Apptoto confirmation state.
📝
Log Sessions
Log EPIC and EI session pairs directly to your CONT tab in Google Sheets from within the app — no manual spreadsheet editing.
🚗
Travel Vouchers
Calculates mileage automatically from Google Calendar event locations. Supports chained trips when you drive session-to-session.
⚠️
Needs Attention
Surfaces unresolved, flagged, and rejected claims from your billing log so nothing slips through the cracks.

Initial Setup

Provider Management connects directly to your Google Sheet via your Google account. Setup takes about two minutes — you only do it once.

📋
Before you begin: You need a copy of the Childhood Interventions spreadsheet template in your Google Drive. Contact support@childhoodinterventions.com if you haven't received it. The sheet must have tabs named: CONT, Ongoing, Humana, Sunshine, Simply, FCC.
1
Copy the spreadsheet template to your Drive
Open the template shared with you and choose File → Make a copy. Save it to your own Google Drive. Do not work in the original shared file.
2
Open Provider Management and sign in with Google
Open Provider Management from the Hub. A setup screen appears on first load. Click Sign in with Google and authorize access using the same Google account that owns your spreadsheet.
3
Paste your Google Sheet URL
Open your copy of the spreadsheet in Google Sheets and copy the URL from your browser's address bar. Paste it into the Sheet URL field in the setup screen — it will look like https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/…
4
Fill in your details and click Get Started
Enter your name, discipline, home address (for travel mileage), and FLEPIC billing rate. Click Get Started — the app will load your caseload and schedule immediately.
🔄
ESDS Reconcile

The ESDS Reconcile tab is the fastest way to keep your CONT billing log in sync with ESDS claim statuses.

1
Download ClaimDetailExtract.xlsx from ESDS
Log into ESDS, navigate to your claim reports, and export the ClaimDetailExtract file. This is the standard billing export format ESDS provides.
2
Drop the file into the Reconcile tab
In Provider Management, click ESDS Reconcile in the sidebar. Drag-and-drop the .xlsx file onto the upload area, or click to browse.
3
Review the diff
The app compares the ESDS data against your CONT tab and shows a color-coded list: new claims not in your sheet, status changes (e.g., approved → paid), and missing ESDS entries.
4
Apply updates
Click Apply All Updates to write the reconciled statuses back to your CONT tab in Google Sheets. Review each change if you prefer — you can apply selectively.

Caseload Tab

The Caseload tab pulls from your Ongoing sheet tab and gives you a live dashboard of every active child — sorted by urgency.

📅
Auth Expiration Tracking
Auth end dates are color-coded — red for expiring within 30 days, yellow for 60 days. Never let an auth lapse by surprise.
🗓️
Review Due Dates
Periodic and Annual review due dates are calculated and displayed for each child so you can plan ahead for IFSP meetings.
📞
Caregiver Contact
Caregiver name, phone, and email are displayed per child — tap to call or email directly from your mobile browser.
📅
Schedule & Calendar

The Schedule tab shows your current week pulled live from Google Calendar. Sessions are color-coded and show Apptoto confirmation status when available.

💡
The calendar reads from the Google account that owns your linked spreadsheet. Use the same calendar for all Early Steps sessions so all events appear here.
📝
Logging Sessions

Use Add Session or Log Session to record EPIC and EI session pairs directly into your CONT tab without opening Google Sheets.

1
Open Add Session
Click Add Session in the sidebar. Select the child from your caseload list or enter their name and auth number manually.
2
Fill in session details
Enter the session date, duration, session type (EPIC or EI), location, and billing codes. Both EPIC and EI entries for the same visit can be logged together.
3
Submit
Click Log Session. The Apps Script writes the new row(s) directly to your CONT tab. You'll see a confirmation when it's saved.
🚗
Travel Vouchers

The Travel Voucher feature auto-calculates mileage from your Google Calendar event locations and generates a printable voucher at the current IRS mileage rate ($0.70/mile for 2025).

1
Ensure Calendar events have addresses
Travel is calculated from the Location field of each Google Calendar event. Add the full street address of each session location to your calendar events for accurate mileage.
2
Open the Travel Voucher tab and select a date range
Choose the pay period you're vouching for. The app reads your calendar events for that range and maps each trip from your home (or prior session location for chained trips).
3
Review calculated miles and generate the voucher
Review the trip list, adjust any legs if needed, then click Generate Voucher. A printable PDF is created showing each trip and the total reimbursable amount.
🔗
Chained trips: If you drove directly from one session to the next (without returning home), the app calculates mileage from the first session address to the next — not from your home — so you aren't credited for mileage you didn't drive.

Saving & Data

Provider Management is a read/write bridge between this app and your Google account. Here's exactly where each piece of data lives:

DataWhere storedPHI?
Sheet ID, name, discipline, home address, billing rateYour browser (localStorage)No
Caseload / Ongoing dataYour Google Sheet (Ongoing tab)Yes
CONT billing logYour Google Sheet (CONT tab)Yes
Calendar eventsYour Google CalendarYes
ESDS reconcile fileLocal browser memory only (not saved)Yes
Travel voucher PDFYour device (download)No
🛡️
HIPAA Note: All PHI (child names, dates, billing data) is stored in your own Google account — not on Childhood Interventions servers. Google Workspace for Healthcare can be used under a Google BAA if your organization requires it. The app connects directly to your Google Sheet using the URL you provide — no data is stored or proxied through our servers. The ClaimDetailExtract file you upload is processed entirely in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers.
