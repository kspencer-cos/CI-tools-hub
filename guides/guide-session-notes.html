Overview

EI Session Notes is a browser-based tool that helps Early Steps providers write, complete, and store ESDS-compliant session notes faster — without sacrificing quality or compliance. The tool uses AI to draft note language based on your session inputs, then lets you review, edit, and finalize before generating a PDF.

🤖
AI-Assisted Drafting
Enter session details and the AI generates ESDS-compliant note language for you to review and edit.
✍️
Caregiver Signatures
Capture a digital caregiver signature directly in the browser — no paper or separate app needed.
☁️
Cloud Roster
Your child roster is stored securely in the cloud — accessible from any device, not just one browser.
📄
PDF Export
Generate a clean, printable PDF of each completed note for your records and upload to ESDS.
📂
Notes History
All completed notes are saved per child so you can review prior sessions at any time.

HIPAA BAA
A Business Associate Agreement is presented and signed before first use — required for HIPAA compliance.

Getting Started

Access EI Session Notes through the Childhood Interventions Provider Hub at tools.childhoodinterventions.com — your email and password are all you need. The hub passes your license key automatically.

1
Sign in to the Provider Hub
Go to tools.childhoodinterventions.com and enter your email and password. Click Session Notes from the dashboard — the tool opens with your license key pre-loaded.
2
Sign the Business Associate Agreement
On first use, you'll be presented with a HIPAA BAA. Enter your full legal name and organization name, check the confirmation box, and click I Agree — Sign and Continue. This is required by law and only done once.
3
Set up your provider profile
Enter your name, credentials (optional), and organization name. This information prints on every note PDF you generate. Click Save and continue.
4
Add your first child to the roster
From the Roster view, click Add Child and enter the child's name and date of birth. You can add more details later. Your roster is stored in secure cloud storage.
💡
Already have a roster? If you previously used the tool and stored children in your browser, you'll be offered the chance to migrate that roster to cloud storage — choose Migrate my roster to make it available on any device.
✍️
Writing a Session Note

Each session note documents an individual visit. The tool walks you through the required fields, then hands the information to AI to draft compliant note language.

1
Select the child from your roster
Tap the child's name on the Roster screen, then choose Start Session. If you have a saved draft, you'll see an option to Resume Draft instead.
2
Fill in session details
Enter the session date, duration, location, EPIC and EI activity details, caregiver coaching provided, and child/family responses. Required ESDS fields are clearly marked.
3
Generate the note
When all required fields are complete, click Generate Note. The AI drafts a compliant session note based on your inputs — this usually takes 10–20 seconds.
4
Review and edit the draft
The generated note appears in a text editor. Read through it carefully, make any corrections or additions, and ensure it accurately reflects the session. You are the clinician — always review AI output.
5
Obtain caregiver signature and finalize
Hand the device to the caregiver to sign, then finalize and export the PDF. The completed note is saved to the child's history in the cloud.
⚠️
Always review AI-generated text. The AI drafts note language based on what you enter — but you are responsible for clinical accuracy. Edit anything that doesn't precisely reflect what happened in the session before finalizing.
✍️
Caregiver Signatures

The tool includes a built-in signature capture pad so caregivers can sign directly on your device — no paper forms or separate signature apps required.

1
Hand your device to the caregiver
After reviewing the completed note, tap Get Signature. A full-screen signature pad appears — give the caregiver your phone or tablet to sign.
2
Caregiver signs with a finger or stylus
The caregiver draws their signature on the pad. If they make a mistake, they can tap Clear and try again.
3
Accept and embed the signature
Tap Accept Signature — the signature is embedded into the note PDF along with the date and time it was captured.
ℹ️
Signatures are captured as image data embedded in the PDF. They are not transmitted or stored separately from the note record. A caregiver who declines to sign should be noted in the session narrative.

Saving & Exporting

Understanding how your notes are saved helps ensure you never lose work. The tool uses a combination of auto-save drafts and permanent cloud storage for completed notes.

WhatWhere it's savedWhenPHI stored?
Session draft Your browser (local) Auto-saved as you type Yes
Child roster Secure cloud (KV store) Immediately on save Yes
Completed notes Secure cloud (KV store) On finalize Yes
Exported PDF Your device (download) On demand Yes
License key Session storage (browser tab) On login No
💡
Draft auto-save: Backing out of the Review & Sign screen with the ← arrow auto-saves a draft of your summary edits, next steps, caregiver names, and progress levels — so in-progress work is never lost. The draft is waiting the next time you start a session for the same child.
📥
Exporting a PDF: After finalizing a note, click Download PDF. Save the file to a HIPAA-compliant location (e.g., your organization's encrypted drive) and upload to ESDS as required. Do not store note PDFs in personal cloud storage (Google Drive personal, Dropbox, iCloud) unless those services have a signed BAA with your organization.

HIPAA & Privacy
🛡️
This tool operates under a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA)
Childhood Interventions, LLC serves as a Business Associate under HIPAA. Before your first session note, you sign a BAA that establishes the legal framework for handling Protected Health Information (PHI). Your organization remains the Covered Entity responsible for patient data under HIPAA.
🔑
Access Control
Access requires a valid license key verified against our server. No key = no access to the tool or any child data.
🗄️
Encrypted Storage
Child rosters and completed notes are stored in an encrypted cloud key-value store. Data is not visible to any other provider or organization.
🌐
Encrypted Transit
All data transferred between your browser and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Never transmitted over unencrypted connections.
🤖
AI & PHI
The AI generation process uses your session inputs. Do not include full child names or SSNs in free-text fields — use initials and child IDs instead.

Your responsibilities as the provider:

  • Use the tool only on devices you control and that have screen-lock enabled.
  • Do not share your license key with anyone outside your organization.
  • Store downloaded PDFs only in HIPAA-compliant locations.
  • Report any suspected unauthorized access to privacy@childhoodinterventions.com immediately.
  • Do not photograph or screen-record session note screens in unsecured environments.

Managing Your Roster

Your cloud roster keeps all the children you serve in one place. Roster data is stored per your license key, so it's available on any device you sign in from.

+
Adding a child
Click Add Child on the Roster screen. Enter the child's name and date of birth. Other details (auth dates, diagnosis, etc.) can be added or edited later.
✏️
Editing a child record
Tap the child's name to open their record. Use the Edit button to update any information. Changes save immediately to the cloud.
📂
Viewing note history
Tap View Notes from any child's card to see all completed notes. You can re-download any prior PDF from this view.
🗑️
Removing a child
Open the child's record and choose Remove from roster. This removes the child from your active roster but does not permanently delete completed note records (retained per your BAA).

Tips & Best Practices
⚡
Use specific inputs
The more specific your session details, the better the AI draft. Include specific activities, targets, and responses — not just "practiced walking."
📱
Works on phones
The tool is fully mobile-responsive. You can complete the entire workflow — entry, AI generation, signature, PDF — on your smartphone.
🔄
Multiple devices
Your roster and completed notes sync across devices. Start a note on your phone in the field; finish it on your laptop at home.
📤
Upload PDFs promptly
Upload completed note PDFs to ESDS within your organization's required timeframe. The tool doesn't auto-submit to ESDS — that step is yours.
